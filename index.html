Recipe for Black Soup

My Favourite meal is Black Soup. Black Soup is a popular and tasteful (Edo) Esan soup, it is quite delicious. I love it and i'm guessing you are going to love it too. I have been exploring food by other Nigerian ethnic groups and today we are going to be making the popular Edo(Esan) Black soup

It really doesn't matter your language or ethnic group, if you try the soup and get it right, you are going to have the same experience as those that have been enjoying it for ages. Most people complain about the color ,it gets the color from the ground leaves.It is super tasty and offers several health benefit. There are three basic leaves for making this delicious soup; bitter leaves,scent leaves(effirin) and uziza leaves.


Ingredients Measurements
Washed bitter leaves 2 cups
sliced scent leaves half cup
sliced uziza leaves 2 cups
roasted fish 300g
assorted meats 1kg
ground crayfish half cup
ground pepper half cup
seasoning cubes 3
stockfish ear 7 pieces
palm fruits sauce 4 cups
salt to taste

servings:6
preparation time: 75 Minutes


How to Cook Black soup

  1. Grind all the leaves together,these ground leaves act as the soup thickner and also gives it the black-looking color,hence the name - black soup. You can blend to paste by adding a little water to ease up the movement of the blades.
  2. Wash and boil the meat with half a cup of onions,1 seasoning cubes and a pinch of salt. Cook for 30=45 minutes, until the meat becomes soft and easily chewable, you can add a little water to stop it from getting burned.
  3. Add the dry fish (hot-water-washed),stockfish,crayfish, add the banga sauce.
  4. Allow to cook for up to 15 minutes until the soupy mixture thickens.
  5. Add the ground leaves,stir,add 2 seasoning cubes,salt to taste, allow to simmer for another five minutes and you are done
  6. Black soup is served with Nigerian swallow (eba, fufu, semo or pounded yam)
